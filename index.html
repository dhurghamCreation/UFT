About UL Global

University of Limerick has been named among the top 500 universities globally, and UL Global supports all international students from pre-arrival to graduation.

A globally focused university community

UL has significantly improved its position in the 2024 QS World University Rankings, climbing more than 100 places to a ranking of 426 overall. UL is now in the top 2% of universities in the world. It is an independent, internationally focused university.

It is a young, energetic and enterprising university with a proud record of innovation in education and excellence in research and scholarship. Located on the west coast of Ireland along the picturesque River Shannon, the university blends a natural setting with state-of-the-art teaching and research facilities.
